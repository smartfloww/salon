— Our Philosophy

A salon should feel less like a transaction, more like a ritual.

Lumière opened in 2012 with a single belief — that great hair is not an accident, and that the path to it should feel as considered as the result.

Our team of nine stylists brings together specialties honed in London, Tokyo and New York. We listen first, and we never reach for the shears until we understand the life your hair has to live.
